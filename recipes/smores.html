Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Milk chocolate Digestive bisquit
  • 2 Large size marshmallows

Steps

  1. Place the cookie (bisquit) on a plate, chocolate-side up. This will be the base of your S'more.
  2. Set the top cookie close by.
  3. Thread two marshmallows on a bamboo skewer or thin stick.
  4. Toast two marshmallows over an open flame until hot & soft in the middle - the marshmallows might get a little droopy (be careful to keep them on the skewer). If they catch on fire, just blow them out and continue toasting until browned.
  5. Slide toasted marshmallows onto the cookie (chocolate side) and sandwich the top cookie on top.
  6. Press down slightly until the marshmallow oozes out the sides a little.
  7. These are best eaten immediately, but are darn good eaten after they cool.
